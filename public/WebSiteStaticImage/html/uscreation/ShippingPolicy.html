At US Creation, we strive to ensure a smooth and efficient shipping process for all our customers. Once an order is confirmed, we begin processing it to prepare for shipment. The estimated timeframe for processing and shipping is between 5 to 7 days from the date of order confirmation. This means that once your order has been successfully placed and verified, it will take approximately this duration before it is dispatched from our warehouse.

Please note that this 5 to 7-day timeframe applies to business days only and does not include weekends or public holidays. If your order is placed on a Friday or right before a public holiday, processing will begin on the next working day. Additionally, there may be circumstances beyond our control, such as extreme weather conditions, supply chain disruptions, or carrier delays, that could affect shipping times. While we make every effort to adhere to the estimated timeframe, unforeseen situations may sometimes cause slight delays.
